Follow Tayblet

Tayblet is a Nigerian Afrobeats and Afropop artist whose infectious sound bridges the streets of Lagos with listeners across the globe. Known for collaborations with some of Nigeria's finest — including Seyi Vibez, Mohbad, and Zinoleesky — Tayblet has carved out a distinctive voice in the African music scene.

With a catalogue that ranges from the laid-back groove of No Rush to the euphoric bounce of Way Up and the cinematic energy of Vibration, every record is a testament to an artist who refuses to be boxed in. The music is honest, the delivery is effortless, and the potential is limitless.
